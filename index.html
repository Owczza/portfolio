About Me

So, my real name is Ola, but I prefer to be called Owca (Oftsa, which means 'Sheep' in Polish). It's been my nickname for about 10 years now, and I honestly love it.

Born and raised in Gdynia, I'm now 22 years old. Being a daughter of an American immigrant I was quite lucky to start my life off bilingual. Not to mention the amazing travel experiences!

I tend to be drawn to the more dark, macabre and grotesque art. There is something so beautifully unnerving about Tim Burton's movies or Tool's music.

If you couldn't tell, I'm big on Alice in Wonderland, too.

I love memes and doggos, and videogames. One of my most recent "discoveries" is coding and front-end development. I love it so much! I'm attending a course to learn more about it all and I couldn't be happier.
